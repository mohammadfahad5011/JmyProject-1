it's time for

peace of mind

Scotts Wright Smart Wills enables you to write your will from the comfort of your own home, giving you time to consider your options, at a time to suit you.

And being part of Scotts Wright, a long established and trusted law firm, you can have complete peace of mind knowing that your will is being reviewed by our expert solicitors, without the need to come into our offices.

If you feel that your estate is complex and have things to consider like business succession plans, trusts and minimising inheritance tax, it may help to first discuss these complexities with our specialist solicitors. We’ll be very happy to hear from you.

Using Scotts Wright Smart Wills is easy. Our online questionnaire asks simple questions regarding assets and property and who you would like to leave them to.

what are the costs?

It costs £100 for a single will prepared through Scotts Wright Smart Wills. Mirror Wills for married couples prepared through Smart Wills costs £150.00. This does not include additional advice or consultation / tax planning. If this is required we will agree fees with you before undertaking such work.
